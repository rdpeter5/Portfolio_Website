AR Design

During my studies, I had the opportunity to take an augmented reality class where we explored the potential of AR technology in design. One of the projects we worked on involved animating posters using Adobe After Effects, then using Adobe Aero to view the printed posters on a mobile device and see the animation come to life in a virtual space.

At first, the concept of augmented reality seemed daunting to me, but as I delved deeper into the project, I began to appreciate the endless possibilities that this technology provides for design. The ability to enhance the user's experience by adding an extra layer of interactivity and depth to a static design is truly remarkable.

Through this project, I gained valuable experience in using Adobe After Effects to create animations, as well as learning the process of exporting and integrating those animations into Adobe Aero. I was able to experiment with different design elements, such as typography, colors, and imagery, to create engaging and visually dynamic posters that came to life through the power of AR.

Overall, this project opened my eyes to the potential of augmented reality in design and sparked my interest in exploring this technology further in future projects. The ability to create an immersive experience for the user through a simple poster is a testament to the power of design and technology working together.
