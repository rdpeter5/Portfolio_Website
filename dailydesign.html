Daily Designs 2022

For the entirety of 2022, I took on the challenge of creating a new design every day using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The goal was twofold: to challenge myself creatively and to develop a consistent art practice. To hold myself accountable, I recorded myself designing each piece and posted a time-lapse video to TikTok, along with the final image to Instagram.

Over the course of the year, I explored a wide range of styles, subjects, and techniques. Some designs were simple and minimalistic, while others were intricate and detailed. I drew inspiration from a variety of sources, including nature, pop culture, and my own personal experiences. Each design was a unique expression of my creativity, and I learned valuable lessons about composition, color theory, and design principles along the way.

Through this project, I discovered the power of consistent practice and the importance of setting achievable goals. By committing to creating a new design every day, I was able to push past creative blocks and develop my skills as an artist. I also gained a following on social media, connecting with other artists and sharing my work with a wider audience. Overall, this project was a transformative experience that helped me grow both as an artist and as a person.
